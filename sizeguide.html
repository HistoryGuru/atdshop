SIZE GUIDE

Find your perfect fit for maximum grip and comfort.

GRIPLOCK PRO socks are designed with a snug compression fit. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up. Measure your foot length while standing for the most accurate result.

Size UK Shoe Size EU Shoe Size US Shoe Size Foot Length
S 3 – 5 36 – 38 4 – 6 22 – 24 cm
M 5.5 – 7.5 38.5 – 41 6.5 – 8.5 24 – 26 cm
L 8 – 10 41.5 – 44 9 – 11 26 – 28 cm
XL 10.5 – 13 44.5 – 47 11.5 – 14 28 – 31 cm

HOW TO MEASURE

  1. Place your foot flat on a piece of paper.
  2. Trace around your foot with a pencil held vertically.
  3. Measure the distance from your heel to the tip of your longest toe.
  4. Match this measurement to the Foot Length column above.
